Who We Are

Born to Challenge Limits

Cyber Invasion Army was founded in 2025 by BUP students driven by curiosity, precision, and a deep passion for cybersecurity.

We live by the Uchiha spirit — discipline, determination, and a relentless will to learn. Every challenge sharpens our skills; every exploit teaches us something new.

We aim to build not just a team, but a legacy of excellence in cybersecurity. Across 70+ CTFs, we've climbed to #49 globally and #3 in Bangladesh on CTFtime's 2026 rankings.

2025 — just the beginning.
